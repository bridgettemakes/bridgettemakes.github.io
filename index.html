LovexMurder is a passion project being created in our spare hours after work and on weekends.

Current target for demo release is Fall 2025.

Right now we are working on art assets and writing, with the highest priority tech already complete. As those assets fill out, this website will reflect those updates.

Planned release to Steam with other consoles under consideration.

Bridgette Kuehn Heuser is the sole developer, artist, and writer of LovexMurder. She had the idea for this game while a few pina coladas deep on vacation ("oh my god what if you could date the murderer") and has been working on the title since that summer of 2023. When she isn't working on this game, you can find her co-hosting the video game podcast, Baddy Breakdowns, or working at her day job at Xbox. LovexMurder will be her debut title.

Maddy Whisenant is the music composer of LovexMurder. When she isn't working on this game, you can find her co-hosting the video game podcast, Baddy Breakdowns, or working at her day job at Xbox. LovexMurder will be her debut title.
